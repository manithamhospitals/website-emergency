What is Vascular Surgery?

Vascular Surgery deals with disorders of blood vessels outside the heart, including arteries, veins, and circulation-related conditions. At Manitham Multispeciality Hospital, we provide timely assessment, treatment planning, and surgical care for patients with vascular problems affecting comfort, mobility, and overall health.

Vascular Surgery Care at Manitham Multispeciality Hospital

  • Evaluation of circulation problems, swelling, and vessel-related pain
  • Care for varicose veins and selected venous conditions
  • Assessment of limb blood flow and peripheral vascular concerns
  • Surgical planning for vascular blockages or complications
  • Follow-up care focused on healing, circulation, and prevention

Facilities

  • Specialist consultation and vascular assessment
  • Imaging, operation theatre support, and monitored inpatient care
  • Recovery planning with wound care and circulation-focused follow-up

Our Vascular Surgery Department Process

  • Vascular symptom review and clinical examination
  • Diagnostic testing to define blood vessel involvement
  • Individualized treatment and procedure planning
  • Safe surgical support with careful monitoring
  • Follow-up to support wound healing and healthy circulation
