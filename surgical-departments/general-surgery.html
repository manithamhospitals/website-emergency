What is General Surgery?

General Surgery is the surgical specialty that focuses on the diagnosis, operative treatment, and post-operative care of a wide range of conditions affecting the abdomen, digestive tract, soft tissues, skin, breast, and emergency surgical cases. At Manitham Multispeciality Hospital, our General Surgery team delivers timely evaluation, planned procedures, and round-the-clock surgical support with a strong focus on patient safety and recovery.

General Surgery Care at Manitham Multispeciality Hospital

  • Hernia evaluation and hernia repair
  • Appendicitis and emergency abdominal surgery
  • Gallbladder stones and gallbladder surgery
  • Piles, fissure, fistula, and anorectal procedures
  • Soft tissue swellings, cysts, abscesses, and lipoma removal
  • Breast lump and minor surgical procedures
  • Wound care, trauma support, and post-surgical follow-up

Facilities

  • Dedicated surgical consultation and pre-operative assessment
  • Operation theatre support for elective and emergency procedures
  • Lab, imaging, anesthesia, and inpatient recovery support

Our General Surgery Department Process

  • Detailed clinical examination and surgical assessment
  • Diagnostic tests and imaging to confirm the condition
  • Clear discussion of surgical and non-surgical treatment options
  • Pre-operative preparation with anesthesia and risk evaluation
  • Safe operative care with close intra-operative monitoring
  • Post-operative pain control, wound care, and recovery guidance
  • Regular follow-up to support healing and prevent complications
