What is Nephrology?

Nephrology focuses on kidney health, including the diagnosis and treatment of kidney diseases, renal failure, electrolyte imbalance, and urinary abnormalities. Early detection is vital as kidney diseases can worsen without intervention. At Manitham Multispecialty Hospital, Perambur, Chennai, has a nephrology team that delivers expert renal assessments, treatment, personalized health advice, and comprehensive support to patients of all ages.

Nephrology Care at Manitham Multispeciality Hospital

  • Chronic kidney disease (CKD)
  • Acute kidney injury / acute renal failure
  • Diabetes-related kidney disease (Diabetic nephropathy)
  • Hypertensive kidney disease
  • Kidney and bladder stones
  • Kidney infections and cysts
  • Glomerulonephritis and nephrotic syndrome
  • Urine abnormalities (Proteinuria / Haematuria)
  • Electrolyte and acid-base disturbances
  • Anaemia and bone disease related to kidney disease
  • Autoimmune kidney diseases (Lupus nephritis, vasculitis)
  • End-stage kidney disease (ESKD)
  • Kidney, urethra, and bladder cancers (evaluation support)

Facilities

  • Kidney function tests and urine analysis
  • Imaging support for stones/infections/cysts
  • Coordinated care with Urology and other specialties

Our Department Process

  • Clinical evaluation and renal risk profiling
  • Kidney function tests and urine analysis
  • Imaging support for stones/infections/cysts
  • Medical management and complication prevention
  • Diet counselling (renal diet) and lifestyle guidance
  • Long-term follow-up for CKD and high-risk patients
  • Coordinated care with Urology and other specialties
