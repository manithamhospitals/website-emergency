What is Gastroenterology?

Gastroenterology is the medical specialty that focuses on the digestive system and its disorders. This includes everything from the esophagus and stomach to the small intestine, colon, liver, gallbladder, and pancreas. Balanced digestive health is key to overall physical well-being. At Manitham Multispecialty Hospital in Perambur, Chennai, our gastroenterology team provides specialized care for various gastrointestinal and liver-related conditions.

Gastroenterology Care at Manitham Multispeciality Hospital

  • Gastritis, acidity, and GERD (reflux)
  • Peptic ulcers and stomach pain
  • Irritable Bowel Syndrome (IBS) and constipation
  • Inflammatory Bowel Disease (IBD) - Crohn's/Colitis
  • Liver diseases including fatty liver and hepatitis
  • Gallstones and bile duct issues
  • Pancreatitis management
  • GI bleeding evaluation and support
  • Digestive system cancer screening support

Facilities

  • Endoscopy and colonoscopy services (coordinated care)
