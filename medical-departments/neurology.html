What is Neurology?

Neurology is the branch of medicine dealing with disorders of the nervous system. The nervous system is a complex, sophisticated system that regulates and coordinates body activities. It has two major divisions: Central nervous system and Peripheral nervous system. At Manitham Multispecialty Hospital in Perambur, Chennai, our neurology department offers comprehensive care for disorders affecting the brain, spinal cord, nerves, and muscles.

Neurology Care at Manitham Multispeciality Hospital

  • Headaches/Migraines management
  • Stroke screening and management (coordinated care)
  • Epilepsy and Seizure disorders
  • Neuropathy (Numbness and Tingling)
  • Vertigo and Balance disorders
  • Parkinson's and other movement disorders
  • Back and Neck pain related to nerves
  • Dementia assessment and support

Facilities

  • Specialized Neurology outpatient care
  • Advanced diagnostic support including CT, MRI, and Lab testing
  • In-patient facilities for complex neurological conditions

Our Department Process

  • Comprehensive neurological history and detailed physical examination
  • Relevant diagnostic assessments (lab work or imaging) for accurate diagnosis
  • Clear explanation of the condition and personalized treatment goal setting
  • Evidence-based medical management and complication prevention
  • Lifestyle guidance and risk factor modification support
  • Long-term care and follow-up to optimize neurological health
