What is Cardiology?

Cardiology is the branch of medicine that focuses on the diagnosis and treatment of heart-related diseases. Heart problems can occur at any age and require accurate diagnosis and timely care to prevent complications. Manitham Multispecialty Hospital is one of the trusted cardiology hospitals in Perambur, Chennai, equipped with essential cardiac diagnostic facilities and expert doctors.

Cardiology Care at Manitham Multispeciality Hospital

  • Chest pain (Angina)
  • Heart attack (Myocardial infarction)
  • Heart failure
  • Valve disorders (MS, MR, AS, AR)
  • Arrhythmias (irregular heartbeat)
  • Pacemaker-related issues
  • Congenital heart diseases (ASD, VSD, CoA)
  • Pulmonary arterial hypertension (PAH)

Facilities

  • Electrocardiogram (ECG) to assess heart activity and valves
  • Two-dimensional echocardiogram (2D ECHO)
  • Cardiac diagnostic facilities

Our Department Process

  • Evaluation of cardiovascular health and risk factors
  • Electrocardiogram (ECG) to assess heart activity and valves
  • Two-dimensional echocardiogram (2D ECHO) to evaluate heart function and valves
  • Expert consultation and diagnosis by our cardiology specialists
  • Personalized care plan, medications, and health recommendations
  • Referrals to specialists for advanced interventions if needed
  • Ongoing monitoring for sustained cardiovascular wellness
