Early Signs of Diabetes – What You Should Never Ignore

Diabetes is one of the most common lifestyle diseases affecting people of all age groups today. It often develops silently, showing mild symptoms that many individuals tend to ignore. Early identification and proper management can prevent serious complications affecting the heart, kidneys, nerves, and eyes.

At Manitham Multispeciality Hospital, Chennai, our Department of Diabetology focuses on early detection, personalized treatment plans, and long-term prevention strategies to help patients live healthier and complication-free lives.

Common Early Symptoms of Diabetes

The early signs of diabetes may appear mild but should never be ignored. Recognizing symptoms at the right time can help in early diagnosis and effective treatment.

  • Frequent urination
  • Increased thirst
  • Unexplained weight loss
  • Extreme fatigue
  • Blurred vision
  • Slow healing wounds

At Manitham Hospital, our Diabetic Control Plan includes complete assessment, drug therapy, insulin management, diet planning, exercise guidance, sleep regulation, and continuous follow-up care to prevent complications.
